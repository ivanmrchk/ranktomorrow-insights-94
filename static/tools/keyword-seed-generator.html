What Are Keyword Seeds?

Keyword seeds are the starting points for professional keyword research. They're broad terms or phrases that you enter into research tools like Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, or SEMrush to discover actual search queries people use.

Unlike finished keyword lists, seeds are designed to unlock discovery. A single seed like "email marketing software" might reveal hundreds of related long-tail keywords with real search volume data.
